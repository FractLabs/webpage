About us

FractLabs is a small dev team building apps to make your life easier. We're starting small by creating some simple apps, but we have big ideas for the future.

So far, we've developed two applications: Fract Passgen, A secure password generator, and Fract Text Editor, A lightweight tool to create and edit text files.

We're always improving our skills, and with every step forward, we're building more ambitious and refined projects. Stay tunned -- the best is yet to come.
